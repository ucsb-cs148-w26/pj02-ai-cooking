#Problem Scenario

##Personas:

John Jones, 19. John can't eat gluten and wants to learn how to cook meals that still taste good. John uses our product and enters in his dietary restrictions as well as inventory of food that he owns. He is then given several recipes that he can cook using food in his kitchen.

Lola Lemon, 47. Lola is trying to eat healthier meals by having a sugar-free diet. Lola uses our product and enters her no sugar restriction. She is then given several recipes that she can cook without sugar.

Emily Rodriguez, 28 years old. Emily is a busy working professional who loves cooking but struggles with several challenges. Emily needs a way to organize her scattered recipes from various sources and track what ingredients she has at home. She wants to reduce food waste by getting reminders before ingredients expire and receive recipe suggestions based on what’s already in her pantry, avoiding unnecessary grocery trips.

Yiyi Du, 17 years old. Yiyi is allergic to tree nuts and sea food, but she has long desired to try what it tastes like of some dishes with these ingredients. So she wants some alternative ingredients to make the food taste like tree nuts and sea food. She is then given several recipes that she can cook using food in her fridge.

Steve Steve, 25. Steve is a professional athlete that has to adhere to a strict diet in order to ensure he is performing at his best. Steve wants to have a clear understanding of what he puts in his body, cooking healthy meals that actually taste good. He wants to make sure he is eating a whole foods diet that has a high protein 
content.

All the personas want to change their diets and learn how to cook for different reasons, whether it is wanting to be gluten free or eating less sugar or trying to control allergies. Our product takes their ingredient inventory and takes into account their restrictions/specifications when providing recipes. Our product is better than the usual recipe apps as it is tailored to the ingredients already in the users’ kitchen. It guarantees that any recipe provided can actually be cooked by the user because it follows their restrictions and uses ingredients they already have.
