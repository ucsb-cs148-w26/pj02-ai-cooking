<h2>1. UI/UX and Visual Design</h2>

<h3>Positive Feedback</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Clean, colorful, vibrant UI</li>
  <li>Login and onboarding pages look good</li>
  <li>Simple and beginner-friendly interface</li>
  <li>Clear categories and layout</li>
</ul>

<h3>Concerns</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Background gradients are distracting or cause lag</li>
  <li>Text readability could be improved</li>
  <li>UI feels like a form or social media app instead of food-related</li>
  <li>Too many steps or pages for recipe generation</li>
  <li>Onboarding may feel too long</li>
  <li>Manual data entry takes too much time</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>2. Pantry Management</h2>

<h3>Positive Feedback</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Core pantry tracking idea is useful and relatable</li>
  <li>Receipt and image scanning features are appreciated</li>
  <li>Expiration tracking is helpful</li>
</ul>

<h3>Concerns and Suggestions</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Need quantity tracking of ingredients</li>
  <li>Need to distinguish fridge vs. freezer</li>
  <li>Need better handling of shared kitchens</li>
  <li>Confirmation before removing expired items</li>
  <li>Highlight or notify about expiring items</li>
  <li>Reduce manual entry time</li>
  <li>Allow batch uploads or faster scanning</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>3. Recipe Generation</h2>

<h3>Positive Feedback</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Recipe suggestions based on pantry items are highly liked</li>
  <li>Personalized recipes (allergies, cuisine) are appreciated</li>
  <li>Concise recipe format is preferred over long blog-style text</li>
</ul>

<h3>Concerns and Suggestions</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Recipes should only use available ingredients</li>
  <li>Add images to recipes</li>
  <li>Allow saving/bookmarking recipes</li>
  <li>Allow modifying generated recipes</li>
  <li>Add calorie or macro filters</li>
  <li>Add time-based filters (e.g., 15-minute meals)</li>
  <li>Provide sources for recipes</li>
  <li>Improve authenticity and accuracy</li>
  <li>Speed up AI response time</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>4. Scanning and AI Features</h2>

<h3>Positive Feedback</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Receipt scanning is considered very useful</li>
  <li>Pantry image scanning is an interesting feature</li>
  <li>AI-generated recipes are engaging</li>
</ul>

<h3>Concerns and Questions</h3>
<ul>
  <li>How accurate is expiration detection?</li>
  <li>Can the system scan multiple items at once?</li>
  <li>Can users edit scanned results?</li>
  <li>Where do recipes come from?</li>
  <li>How reliable is the OCR/image recognition?</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>5. Personalization and Preferences</h2>

<h3>Positive Feedback</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Allergy and dietary preference features are highly valued</li>
  <li>Onboarding customization is appreciated</li>
</ul>

<h3>Suggestions</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Add more dietary options (e.g., halal)</li>
  <li>Allow free-text preferences</li>
  <li>Learn from user feedback over time</li>
  <li>Recipe rating system to improve recommendations</li>
</ul>

<h2>6. Performance and Technical Concerns</h2>

<h3>Concerns</h3>
<ul>

  <li>Manual pantry entry is time-consuming</li>
  <li>Background effects may reduce performance</li>
